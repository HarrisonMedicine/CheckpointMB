Before you start

This is educational information, not a diagnosis. It's built from CancerCare Manitoba's BreastCheck, CervixCheck and ColonCheck guidelines and Diabetes Canada's screening guideline, for adults at average risk with no symptoms.

It doesn't replace an invitation letter from a screening program, and it can't account for every personal or family history detail — bring your answers to your doctor.

Your answers are saved only in this browser tool, so you can pick up where you left off or add reminders to your own calendar.

Not medical advice. Screening needs vary by personal and family history. Always confirm timing and eligibility with a healthcare provider.
